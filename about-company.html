About us

Who we are

Grasyd Enterprises (pvt) LTD trading as GREENFLOW RENEWABLE ENERGY SOLUTIONS is a Privately owned company that was registered in Zimbabwe under the laws of Zimbabwe in December 2013. GREENFLOW has over the years been concentrating mainly on Domestic and Commercial Electrical Installations for both Domestic, General Industrial and Domestic Electrical repairs and maintenance, Repairs to Electrical Appliances, Electric Borehole Installations , Repair and maintenance and many other jobs within the Electrical industry.

We are committed to building a sustainable future by fostering a collaborative spirit that creates exceptional experiences, balanced relationships, and community built environment. Providing green energy is more than a job. It is our passion. With each project we take on, we raise the bar and strive to give our clients the best.
